Cyan/Magenta C#/P#
THAT TF EMA Cross

EXAMPLE: Cyan C30 = 30min EMA Cross
EXAMPLE: Magenta P30 = 30min EMA Cross



Cyan/Magneta CALL#/PUT#
THAT TF EMA Cross WITH Higher TF TREND

EXAMPLE: Cyan CALL15 = 15min EMA cross, 30min is Bullish
EXAMPLE: Magenta PUT15 = 15min EMA cross, 30min is Bearish




Green/Red Call#/Put#
Lower TF MACD cross (0line) WITH TREND

EXAMPLE: Green Call30 = 15min MACD Cross, 30min is bullish
EXAMPLE: Red Put30 = 15min MACD cross, 30min is Bearish
